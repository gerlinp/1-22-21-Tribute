Jean-Jacques Dessalines

The First Emperor of Haiti

  • Born Jean-Jacques Duclos was born into slavery on Cormier, a plantation near Grande-Riviere-du-Nord His father had adopted the surname from his owner Henri Duclos.
  • Working in the sugarcane fields as a laborer, Dessalines worked on the Duclos's plantation until he was about 30 years of age. He was bought by a by a free black man name Dessalines, who assigned his own surname to him. From then on he was called Jean-Jacques Dessalines.
  • He worked for that master for about three years, until the slave uprising of 1791, which spread across the Plaine du Nord.
